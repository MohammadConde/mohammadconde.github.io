About Mohamed Condé

Mr. Mohamed Condé is a dynamic entrepreneur and business leader based in Guinea, West Africa, with a diversified portfolio spanning agricultural exports, infrastructure development, mining, international trade, and strategic investments.

A visionary and self-made leader, he has built and scaled businesses from the ground up, demonstrating strong strategic insight, disciplined execution, and a commitment to long-term value creation.

Under his leadership, Golf Guinea Investment SA is rapidly transforming into a leading enterprise with a growing presence across Africa, Europe, the GCC, and India. His work focuses on sustainable industrial growth, job creation, economic development, and building globally competitive businesses rooted in Africa.

Leadership Journey

From founding international trading ventures in 2012 to building a diversified investment group with operations spanning agriculture, mining, infrastructure, logistics, energy, and global trade, Mohamed Condé's journey reflects a relentless commitment to innovation, industrial development, and long-term value creation across Africa and beyond.

Since founding his first international trading venture in 2012 and establishing Golf Guinea Investment SA in 2015, Mohamed Condé has built a diversified business group spanning agriculture, infrastructure, mining, logistics, renewable energy, and international trade, creating a foundation for long-term growth across Africa and global markets.
